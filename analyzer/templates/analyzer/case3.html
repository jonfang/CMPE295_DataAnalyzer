Oakland Crime Rate Analysis

Is Oakland one of the most dangerous places in America?

Multiple news sources that indicate Oakland city is among one of the most dangerous places in America. We draw data sources from government for the Oakland city crime rate from 2011 and 2016, over 1,000,000 of incidents to gain some insights about why Oakland city is dangerous. Surprisingly, while Oakland has many types of actual crimes such as stolen vehicles, battery, etc. The top alert and crime incidents are actually quite harmless (alarm ring, security check, and 911 hang up) and constitute about 50 percent of the crime report. We can conclude that while Oakland is relatively unsafe, its status as one of the most dangerous city is not completely true based on statistics and our analyzed result.

Online data source: https://www.cbsnews.com/
